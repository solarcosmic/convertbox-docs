Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Sunday 18 May 2025

1. Who We Are

Interlabs develops and maintains the BetterKMR Chrome extension.
Contact: interlabsofficial@gmail.com.
Website: interlabs-official.github.io.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect anonymous telemetry data, including:

  • Which settings are enabled or disabled
  • Which themes are in use

No personal, sensitive, or identifying information is collected at any time.

3. How We Use Data

We use telemetry data to:

  • Improve extension features and functionality
  • Prioritize popular themes and features
  • Identify underused or overused features for future development

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. All data is stored securely on our servers and accessed only by Interlabs.

5. Data Retention

Telemetry data is retained indefinitely, or removed at our own discretion, to help us track long-term trends and improve the extension.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to secure your data, including restricted access and secure storage.

7. User Rights

As we do not collect any personal or identifying data, we cannot fulfill requests to access, modify, or delete individual data. All telemetry is aggregated and anonymous.

8. Opting Out

You may opt out of telemetry data collection at any time via the extension’s settings. You are also prompted to disable telemetry upon install.

9. Children’s Privacy

BetterKMR is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact us at interlabsofficial@gmail.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at our disclosure. Changes will be notified upon inside our extension.
